Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Data We Collect

This website does not store, log, or transmit any personal data. All checks (IP detection, WebRTC, DNS) are performed client-side in your browser. No data is sent to our servers.

Third-Party APIs

To detect your public IP and location, we query ipapi.co and ipify.org. For DNS leak detection, we query cloudflare-dns.com (Cloudflare's public DNS-over-HTTPS endpoint). These services may log your IP per their own privacy policies.

Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out at google.com/settings/ads.

Cookies

We do not set cookies. Google AdSense may set cookies for ad personalization.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact form at the bottom of our main page.
